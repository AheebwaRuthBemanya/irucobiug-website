Background

IRUCOBI is a CBO that works towards a just society where children live complete and acceptable lives in transformed societies and contribute to their development.

IRUCOBI has three main thematic areas that include Health, Child Protection and Education and Livelihood. Our 3-prong model of Rescue, Train and Empower guides implementation of these thematic areas.

IRUCOBI UGANDA is mainly operating in Kisiita Sub county and Kisiita Town Council although some other interventions go beyond the geographical boundaries of these areas.

IRUCOBI has implemented interventions in Child protection, Nutrition advocacy, Budget monitoring and expenditure tracking (BMET), Vocational skills training, free distribution of vegetable seeds and household items to vulnerable community members, supported HIV/AIDS non-suppressing children, training of champions in Communal food granaries system, formation of saving groups (SACSD), supported primary schools to establish backyard gardens among many others.

With funding from Uganda Child Rights NGO Network (UCRNN), IRUCOBI supported Kisiita Town Council to develop a bylaw on Child Safeguarding, ending Child marriage and Teenage Pregnancy. Additionally, IRUCOBI in partnership with World Vision, Right2Grow project has supported Kakumiro District Local government to draft a Food Security and Nutrition Policy aiming at eliminating malnutrition in children under five years.

About us

Vision

IRUCOBI works for a just future where young people live complete and acceptable lives and contribute to the development of their communities.

Mission:

Develop underserved communities by empowering children and youth with appropriate sustainable skills to meet their economic, social and spiritual needs to improve their living conditions and those of their families.

Goal:

children and young people fully equipped with more appropriate sustainable life skills to exploit their full potential living in better transformed society

OUR CORE VALUES

Humanity, Equity, Honesty, Diligence

Our Objectives

  • To empower communities to drive their own sustainable development by strengthening their skills, knowledge and resources
  • To develop rural communities and contribute to the development of children and young people in achieving their complete lives, intellectually, physically, spiritually and economically as individuals.
  • Initiating and promoting social, moral, spiritual and economic development in underserved communities
  • Network with other local, national and international stakeholders to foster development in underserved communities
  • Facilitate communication, information and sensitization for behavioral and mindset change in rural transformation.
  • Promote awareness campaigns and good practices in governance and other related concerns that hinder community transformation.

Our Projects

Skills Training and Enhancement Project (STEP)

Equip youth and adolescent girls and young women with vocational skills to enhance economic development and job creation

Greater Kisiita sub county in Bugangaizi East Constituency in Kakumiro District

1024 youth and women benefited directly from 256 households

Saving and Credit Schemes for Development (SACsD)

The project aims at improving household income to overcome issues of vulnerability by accessing self-managed loans for education, medical and other home related needs. It also encourages households to preserve food in communal granaries for eradicate food insecurity.

Greater Kisiita sub county in Bugangaizi East Constituency in Kakumiro District

330 members are involved in the project saving 16,500 kg of Maize and 26,400kg of beans every after 6 months.
